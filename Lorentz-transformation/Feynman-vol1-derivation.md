# Deriving Lorentz-transformations : Feynman's approach
# ලොරෙන්ට්ස් පරිණාමන ව්‍යුත්පන්න කිරීම: ෆයින්මන්ගේ ප්‍රවේශය

<details open>
<summary><b>English</b></summary>
In *The Feynman Lectures on Physics*, Volume I Chapter 15[1], an approach for deriving the consequences of special relativity is presented. We find this to be a pedagogically useful method due to its simplicity and its direct connection to the famous Michelson–Morley experiment.
<!--, conducted in Cleveland, Ohio, by Albert A. Michelson and Edward W. Morley.-->

By proceeding further in this path, which is somewhat a reverse-engineering, we arrive at the usual set of Lorentz transformations presented in most text books.
</details>
